# PlainCo

**Software that just works.**

We are fed up with paywalls and tricks and a world where nothing just works. I am tired of having to not trust anything on the internet. I want technology that just works. I don't want to read 24 pages of terms and conditions before opening an app to understand if it's going to steal my identity.

We will imagine a better world and just build it, one app at a time.

---

## Golden Rule!!!

*Do unto others as you would have them do unto you.*

This is a guiding principle for us where the es*hitification grows constantly.

## What we stand for!

We will **just** build apps that require minimal rights.

We will collect the minimal amount of data.

We will have minimal ads.

We will sell you the app if you want it, it will have some extra stuff.

We will have feedback form that will push GitHub bugs.

We will eventually build a subscription model that is honest for everyone, and it will not mean that apps that are bought before will lose anything, it will just be a bundle.

## What we are against!

We are against taking advantage of people that don't want to read and just want things to work on the first try.

## The patterns we are not!

You know the ones. We all do.

- A household-name streaming service added ads to a tier its users had already paid for — then sold them a second paid tier to remove the ads it had just introduced.
- Another major streaming service keeps raising its price every year, shrinking its library behind flashy new additions, and splitting its plans into tiers no one asked for.
- A service treats its paying users like a churn statistic instead of like people.

We will not do any of these. If we cannot keep a promise, we break it cleanly and tell you. We do not monetize our way out of one.

## Open Source

We will have as much of the code as possible visible to everyone on this org.

## Fine Print!

There isn't any, that's the whole point.
